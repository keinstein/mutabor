Reserves the first free channel.

That is usually the longest unused one.

Zu beachten
This can change if certain Channels are registered to be sustained. In that case they are postponed until no channels are availlable anymore.

Sustaining is not handled by this class.

Rückgabe
number of the first free channel
Rückgabewerte
end()if no free or sustained channel can be found
